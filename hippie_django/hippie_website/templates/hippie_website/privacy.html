Privacy Policy

What we collect via Google Analytics, why, and how to change your choice.

What we collect

When you accept the cookie banner, HIPPIE uses Google Analytics 4 to collect a pseudonymous, cookie-based client identifier, the pages you visit, an approximate location derived from your IP address (the IP address itself is not stored), and basic browser/device information. This data is not used to identify you personally.

Back to top

Why we collect it

We use this data only to understand aggregate usage patterns — for example, which pages and features are used most — so we can improve HIPPIE.

Back to top

Legal basis

Analytics data is only collected on the basis of your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), given by clicking "Accept" on the cookie banner. If you decline, no analytics data is collected, and the site works exactly the same either way.
